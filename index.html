Hi there!

Welcome to my website :)

The above animation of a binary fractal tree isn't a video or a gif: it was drawn and animated on your browser using the p5.js javascript library. This allows every user to use the computing power of their own devices to render such sketches, instead of relying on the limited computing power of the server this website is hosted on. By leveraging the computing power of each user's device, I can make interactive animations that run in real time on their web browser. Isnt't that just wonderful!?

My goal with this website is to explore the power of such animations to communicate complex ideas: the science of "stuff" - everything from trees to metals to subatomic particles - expressed in the language of computer generated art on javascript canvases. Fortunately, I get to stand on the shoulders of several awesome people who have inspired me to use animations to communicate ideas. Here are some examples:

  • Daniel Shiffmann - The Coding Train
  • Grant Sanderson - 3 blue 1 brown
  • Nicky Case - ncase.me
