  • Golden Spirals

This is a golden spiral themed starting animation for a website frontpage. The speeds of the different steps can be changed, and the animation fits to the size of the window. The content can be given within the boxes drawn after the initial spiral animation. All the colors can be changed. A section of the golden spiral has been left behind in the left bottom corner as an aesthetic design element. This animation is not compatible for vertical screens yet, but that feature will be soon added. Reload the webpage to see the animation again.
