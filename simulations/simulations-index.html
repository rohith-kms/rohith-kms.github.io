Team IISc Bangalore: International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) competition

2017

Represented IISc at the International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) competition. I was part of the hardware team and we developed the Growth Curve and Optical Density device (GCODe), a device to automate the regular measurement of bacterial populations in a culture to generate growth curves. We made two devices - GCODe Mini and Pro. The Pro was still in the development stage at the time of the iGEM conference in Boston, but the Mini was production ready, with a readily available DIY kit and assembly instructions on the website. GCODe was a part of the iGEM IISc 2017 team's larger project, iFLOAT. The project was nominated for the best hardware prize, and won a gold medal at the conference.
